Best movies of all time

Top 3 movies

Avengers

The Avengers (2012) is a superhero film where Earth's mightiest heroes—Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye—team up to stop Loki, Thor's brother, from conquering Earth using a powerful alien army. Directed by Joss Whedon, the movie blends action, humor, and epic battles.

Big hero 6

*Big Hero 6* (2014) is an animated superhero film by Disney, set in the futuristic city of San Fransokyo. The story follows Hiro Hamada, a young robotics prodigy, who forms a bond with Baymax, a gentle, inflatable healthcare robot created by his late brother, Tadashi. After a tragic event, Hiro and Baymax team up with four friends—GoGo, Wasabi, Honey Lemon, and Fred—to form a high-tech superhero group. Together, they work to uncover a illain's plot involving stolen nanotechnology. The film blends action, humor, and emotional depth, exploring themes of friendship, loss, and innovation.

Sector 36

Sector 36 is a gripping crime thriller directed by Aditya Nimbalkar and starring Vikrant Massey and Deepak Dobriyal. The film is based on the horrific Nithari killings of 2006, where a wealthy businessman and his servant were implicated in the gruesome murders of women and children in Noida. Massey plays Prem Singh, the servant and main perpetrator, while Dobriyal portrays a relentless police inspector investigating the case. The film delves into the complexities of the investigation, power dynamics, and moral dilemmas surrounding the brutal crimes. It has received praise for its intense performances and dark, chilling atmosphere. It is available on OTT platforms as of September 2024.
